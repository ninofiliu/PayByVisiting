Hello visitor.

Simply by being here, you have got the power to help a student earn money without giving any kind of personal information, without watching any ad, and without having to give actual money.

You only have to turn this switch on.

computing


Here is what happens when you click on the switch.

This website is hosted by a student. Let's call him John Doe. John is studying computer science so he often have to have a whole lot of computing power available - and that can cost a lot. When a visitor turns the switch on, he allows part of his computer's power to be dedicated to John's tasks. Like this, John can save up the money he invested in computing power to buy more useful things like gluten-free avocado toasts.
